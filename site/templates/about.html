ABOUT

Clear explanations. Visible sources.

Hardwired Stickman turns behavioural research into accessible visual explanations.

What we cover

Human behaviour, animal behaviour, social instincts, evolutionary trade-offs and the systems that shape everyday decisions.

Editorial standards

Each episode should distinguish established evidence from interpretation. Primary research and reputable reviews are preferred. Claims that remain uncertain should be labelled as uncertain.

Corrections

Material errors should be corrected on the episode page and, where appropriate, in the associated video description or pinned comment.

Use of artificial intelligence

Automation may assist with formatting, asset generation and publishing. Research claims and sources should still be checked by a human before publication.
